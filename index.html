  • Khoj-e-Khas (Treasure Hunt):
  • •All teams must have at least 3 members and no more than 4 members.
  • •Time Duration: will be announced before the commencement of each round.
  • •In total there will be three rounds and certain dues will be given to reach the final solution.
  • •First and Second rounds will be elimination round.
  • •Teams must stay together during the Treasure Hunt and are encouraged to work together to solve the clues and retrieve the coins from each location.
  • •Each team will be given a “Clue Booklet” at the beginning of the treasure hunt. All of the treasure hunt clues will be in the booklet. It will be up to the teams to decipher the clues and safely acquire the coin from each of the locations.
  • •Each clue once solved will lead you to a location where you will be able to collect a “Treasure Hunt Coin” Teams will be scored on the total value of the coins collected and the amount of time taken. Once all coins are collected teams must return to the finish in order to be counted as finished. Coin values will be determined based on the difficulty of the clue.
